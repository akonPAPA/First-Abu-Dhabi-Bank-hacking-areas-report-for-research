Assessment rule

A public fact is not treated as a vulnerability by itself. The analysis considers whether several facts can be combined into a credible pretext and whether an internal control would stop that pretext from authorising a payment, account change or sensitive disclosure.

Evidence limitations

The table records pages available on the stated access date. Web content, staff roles, contact details and published reports may change. Screenshots used in the presentation must be checked for private browser data before being committed or submitted.
