Public repository privacy rule

Student identification numbers and the second group member’s personal details are not stored in this public repository. They must be added only to the private LMS submission.

Research boundary

This work uses passive public-source research. It does not include vulnerability scanning, port scanning, credential testing, login attempts, exploitation, phishing simulation, access to private systems or collection of leaked information. The report evaluates possible cyberfraud exposure and does not claim that FAB has a confirmed technical vulnerability.
