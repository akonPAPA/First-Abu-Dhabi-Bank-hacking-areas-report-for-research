Use Arial throughout the PowerPoint file. Keep the same font family and a restrained size hierarchy. Do not place paragraphs on slides. Use short statements, evidence labels, tables and diagrams. Do not display private browser tabs, cookies, account details, student IDs, private messages or unrelated personal information.

Every screenshot must include a small source label and access date. Industry statistics must be identified as sector-level evidence and must not be presented as FAB-specific incident data.

The recording is complete only after the exported MP4 has been played from start to finish, the face and slides are visible, the sound is clear, the duration is between five and seven minutes and the file size is below the LMS limit.
